About Us

Roses Flower Shop opened on January 1, 2018 and is located at #5 Sta Monica, Kapitolyo, Pasig Roses are oftentimes called the Queen of Flowers. And with great merit, this flower has gained this esteemed distinction because of its unfading charm and beauty. Throughout centuries, no other bloom can outrank or outshine rose blossoms. Since, time immemorial, roses have been labeled the flowers of love and romance. Even Greek and Roman mythologies have attributed the beauty of the rose to the Goddess of Love. Victorians have used the rose as a communication device between lovers. The number of blooms given carries a special and specific meaning. Aside from the number, roses also carry special meanings depending on the colors. Red is usually for love, yellow for friendship, pink for gratitude, orange for excitement, and purple for enchantment or love at first sight. This is how courtship ensued in the classical times.
